##  Overview

This project is a **Sales Analysis Dashboard** developed using **Power BI** to analyze business performance across different categories, regions, customers, and time periods.
It provides actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making.

---

##  Objectives

* Analyze overall sales, profit, and cost
* Identify top-performing categories and products
* Track monthly sales trends
* Understand customer contribution to revenue
* Compare regional performance

---

##  Key Metrics

* **Total Sales:** 848K
* **Total Profit:** 162K
* **Total Cost:** 686K
* **Return Rate:** 10%

---

##  Dashboard Features

### 1. Sales Trend Analysis

* Monthly sales visualization
* Identifies peak and low-performing months

### 2. Category-wise Performance

* Comparison of sales across categories:

  * Electronics (Top performer)
  * Home Appliances
  * Books
  * Toys
  * Clothing (Lowest performer)

### 3. Regional Analysis

* Sales distribution across:

  * Central, East, West, North, South

### 4. Customer Insights

* Top 5 customers contributing to YTD sales
* Helps identify key revenue drivers

### 5. KPI Cards

* Quick overview of important business metrics
